Anyone selling new and genuine products is welcome. To start selling, you need to have the following:

  1. PAN Card

  2. GST/GSTIN Number (not mandatory for tax exempted categories)

  3. Bank account and supporting KYC documents (ID Proof, Company Address proof, Warehouse Address proof and Cancelled cheque)

  4. Brand Authorization Letter/Trademark Certificate

  1. Register yourself at seller.paytm.com & submit the required documents. To manage your business smoothly you will be trained on using the Seller panel.

  2. List your products under specific product categories & go live.

  3. Manage orders (Receive orders, pack the product and Request for Pickup. Our courier partner will pick up the product and deliver it to the customer).

  4. Once an order is successfully delivered to the customer, Paytm Mall will initiate your payment within the next working day of the bank.
